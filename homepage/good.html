My history

I was the senior vice president and general manager at Willow Cove from 2012 to 2017. I was responsible for global strategy, marketing and general consulting for Willow Cove's involvement as a global investment bank. I ensured that the company was in a business leadership position and standing out in the growingly competitive market. Prior to my involvement with Willow Cove, I was working at CannonLake as a general manager and strategic partner in bringing out competitive product and marketing stradegy.
